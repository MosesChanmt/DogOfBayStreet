About Us

A Toronto -Canadian owned business which advocates one mission: There is no ugly man. Only lazy man. We are dedicated to serve starting or leading working professionals to beef up their wardrobe game. Inspired by the Hollywood financial movie, the founder believes that even the underdog can steal the day with the right shoes on feet. Before the last whistle blew, everyone could be a dark horse in the game. Let alone the underdog can rule Bay Street oneday , striding proudly down the alley with their remarkable wardrobe trophies.

Our Visions

We envision a world where every professional can stride confidently with style, knowing that their shoes reflect their ambition and personality. We believe that the right pair of smart casual shoes can transform an ordinary day into an extraordinary one, turning heads and making a statement with every step.

Our mission is to empower you to seize each opportunity with confidence and flair. Whether you’re breaking new ground or climbing to the top, our shoes are designed to be more than just footwear—they’re a key to unlocking your potential and showcasing your unique style.

Together, let’s make every step a step toward success and every day a chance to shine. Here’s to making your mark on Bay Street and beyond.
